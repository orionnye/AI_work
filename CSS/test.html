Kapalua

Kapalua is a census-designated place (CDP) in Maui County, Hawaiʻi, United States. A resort development by the Maui Land & Pineapple Company extends inland from Kapalua Bay and Honolua Bay.

Kapalua has a view of the islands of Molokaʻi and Lānaʻi.
